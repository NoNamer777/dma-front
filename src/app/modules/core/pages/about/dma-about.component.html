About

The Dungeons and Dragons Management Application or DnD Mapp for short will be your 1 stop shop for all your Dungeons & Dragons needs. We all know that it sometimes is a lot that you need to manage while playing a game of D&D, DnD Mapp will solve that.

This application strives to accomplish a couple of thing:

  • Look up information about the game mechanics, spells, and everything you'll need to know to play a game with your friends, all under your thumb and in one application.
  • Create Characters and manage their statistics, inventory, their pet(s) or land that they own, and much more.
  • Keep track of the battles! Instantly see where the enemies are, if they are discovered, and plan epic moves with your teammates to defeat them. Confidently sling a Fireball into a group of Wargs now that you know that your friends are standing on a safe distance.
  • Share and keep track of all the knowledge gathered in the story and have all your notes for one particular party conveniently collected in one place.
  • Create encounters or build worlds, and keep all your notes, lore and information about that world in one place. Invite players to participate in your stories, control their battle map, and watch your story unravel in front of their eyes.
